Different Dragon Forms

Dragons have many different forms. Some have wings while some lack them. Some live in the water while others can't. Some have scales while others can have feathers or even be made of bone. Some can live underground while others are forced to live above ground. Some can even be blind while most can see.

  • Winged
  • Swimming
  • Underground
  • Undead

Dragon Types/Names

Dragons come in many different shapes and sizes. They all get their own form and from their form they get a name. This name can be based off their physical features. Or they can be based off something completely different like words that would describe them in different languages such as quetzalcoatl. They can be named off their height like how some are named after how small they are. They can be named off where they were found as well.

  • Quetzalcoatl
  • Dragonnet
  • Hydra
  • Wyrm
