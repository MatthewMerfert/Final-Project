Dragon Habitats

Dragons can live basically anywhere. Some live in forests and jungles. Others can live in lakes or oceans. Some can live in the desert or underground. Some live in the skys. There are even some that live in areas that can be considered unhabitable such as in volcanoes.

Dragon Habitat Features

Dragons gain different features depending on the habitat they live in. It shows how well dragons adpat to their environment. It goes to the point as to where they can adapt no matter their age. They can be born adapted to the knew environment they live in. And even really old dragons can adapt to live in new environments by gaining or losing features.
